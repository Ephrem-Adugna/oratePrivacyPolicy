Privacy Policy for orate.ai
Effective Date: 1/14/2025
orate.ai is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.
1. Information We Collect
* Audio Recordings: We collect and store audio recordings that you create in the app for the purpose of providing feedback and improving your speaking skills.
* Optional User Data: If you choose, you may allow us to store your recordings and associated data in the cloud for easy access across devices.
2. How We Use Your Information
* Feedback: To analyze your recordings and provide you with insights such as transcripts, prosody ratings, and more.
* User Access: To store your recordings securely so you can access them anytime, if you opt in for cloud storage.
* Improving Features: To enhance app functionality and user experience.
3. Data Sharing
* We do not sell or share your personal information, audio recordings, or any other data with third parties.
4. Data Storage and Security
* Audio recordings and associated data are securely stored using encryption.
* Users can delete their recordings and data permanently at any time from within the app.
5. User Control
* Opt-In Storage: Users can choose whether their recordings are stored in the cloud or only locally on their devices.
* Deletion: Users can delete their data, including recordings, transcripts, and feedback, at any time.
6. Permissions
The app requires access to your device's microphone to record audio. This access is only active while you are recording, and no audio is recorded or transmitted without your consent.
7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here, and the effective date will be updated.
